About GreenFit Hub

GreenFit Hub is a free Islamic fitness and health tools website created to help people monitor their health in a simple and easy way. Our goal is to provide useful online calculators that anyone can use without registration, login, or technical knowledge.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote a healthy lifestyle by offering accurate and fast health tools. We believe that taking care of health is an important responsibility, and technology can make this task easier for everyone.

Tools Available on GreenFit Hub

BMI Calculator: The Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator helps users understand whether their weight is healthy according to their height. It is useful for fitness tracking and general health awareness.

Age Calculator: This tool allows users to calculate their exact age in years by entering their birth year. It is helpful for official use, planning, and personal records.

Daily Water Intake Calculator: Staying hydrated is essential for good health. This calculator estimates the amount of water you should drink daily based on your body weight.

Why Choose GreenFit Hub?

  • 100% free and easy to use
  • No signup or personal data required
  • Mobile-friendly and fast loading
  • Clean and Islamic-friendly design

GreenFit Hub is continuously improving and more helpful tools will be added in the future Insha’Allah.
