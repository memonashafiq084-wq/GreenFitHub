Free Fitness & Health Calculators

GreenFitHub is a free Islamic fitness and health tools website designed to help users calculate important health values quickly and accurately. Our tools are simple, mobile-friendly and safe to use. You can calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI), determine your exact age, and estimate your daily water intake without signup. These tools are useful for anyone who wants to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) helps you understand whether your weight is healthy compared to your height. This calculator gives instant results and is useful for tracking your fitness progress.

Age Calculator

The age calculator allows you to quickly calculate your age in years. Simply enter your birth year and get instant results.

Daily Water Intake Calculator

Drinking enough water is essential for good health. This tool estimates how much water you should drink daily based on your weight.
